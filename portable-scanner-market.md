<p><p>The portable scanner market encompasses various types such as laser scanners, linear imagers, and 2D imagers. Laser scanners use laser beams to capture high-precision data and are ideal for quick, accurate scans in various environments. Linear imagers, on the other hand, utilize a single row of sensors to read barcodes and are suited for linear scanning tasks. 2D imagers offer advanced image capture capabilities, enabling them to read both 1D barcodes and 2D codes, making them versatile for diverse applications.</p></p>
