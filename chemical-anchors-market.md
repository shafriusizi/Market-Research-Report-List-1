<p><strong>The Chemical Anchors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injectable Adhensive Anchors</li><li>Capsule Adhensive Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical anchors are essential for providing strong and durable fastening solutions in construction and engineering. Injectable adhesive anchors are liquid adhesives injected into drilled holes, allowing for versatile applications and strong bonding with various substrates. They cure in place, offering excellent load-bearing capabilities. On the other hand, capsule adhesive anchors involve pre-packaged capsules containing adhesive components that are mixed upon installation. This method ensures precise dosing and effective bonding, making them suitable for quick and efficient anchoring tasks.</p></p>
