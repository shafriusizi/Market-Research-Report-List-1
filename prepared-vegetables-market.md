<p><p>The prepared vegetables market encompasses three main types: Ready to Eat (RTE), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Cook (RTC). RTE products are fully cooked and can be consumed straight from the package, offering maximum convenience. RTH items require minimal heating before consumption, making them quick and easy meal options. RTC vegetables are pre-prepared and may require further cooking or processing, allowing for more flexibility in meal preparation while saving time on chopping and washing.</p></p>
