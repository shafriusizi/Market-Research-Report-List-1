<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Barriers</li><li>Electromechanical Barriers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Boom Barrier System market is divided into two main types: hydraulic barriers and electromechanical barriers. Hydraulic barriers utilize fluid power to control the movement of the boom, offering high durability and smooth operation, ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, electromechanical barriers rely on electric motors and gear systems for boom movement, providing quicker response times and easier installation in lighter traffic areas. Both types enhance security and traffic management in various settings, including parking lots and access control points.</p></p>
