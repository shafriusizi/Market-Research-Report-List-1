<p><p>The Cell Harvesting Device market is segmented into four main types: Umbilical Cord, Bone Marrow, Peripheral Blood, and Adipose Tissue. Umbilical Cord devices collect stem cells from umbilical cord blood. Bone Marrow harvesting involves extracting stem cells directly from the bone, often used for transplants. Peripheral Blood collection involves mobilizing stem cells into the bloodstream for easier extraction. Adipose Tissue harvesting utilizes fat tissue, an abundant source of stem cells, for regenerative therapies. Each type serves distinct therapeutic applications.</p></p>
