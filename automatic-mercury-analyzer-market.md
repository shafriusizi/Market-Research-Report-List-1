<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Mercury Analyzer market encompasses two primary types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF). CVAA utilizes a cold vapor technique to measure mercury concentrations by absorbing light at specific wavelengths, making it effective for various sample types. Conversely, CAF employs fluorescence principles to detect mercury levels, offering higher sensitivity and lower detection limits. Both technologies cater to environmental monitoring, industrial applications, and compliance testing, playing a critical role in ensuring mercury safety and regulation adherence.</p></p>
