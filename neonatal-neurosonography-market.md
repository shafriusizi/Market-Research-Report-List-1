<p><p>The neonatal neurosonography market comprises various imaging types, including 2D, 3D, 4D, and Doppler imaging. 2D imaging provides basic structural information, allowing for the assessment of brain anatomy. 3D imaging offers a volumetric view, enhancing spatial understanding of cerebral structures. 4D imaging adds real-time visualization, capturing dynamic processes within the brain. Doppler imaging focuses on blood flow assessment, crucial for identifying vascular issues. Together, these modalities support effective diagnosis and management of neonatal neurological conditions.</p></p>
