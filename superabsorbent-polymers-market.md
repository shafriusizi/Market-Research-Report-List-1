<p><p>The superabsorbent polymers (SAP) market comprises three primary types: starch-based SAP, cellulose-based SAP, and acrylic resin SAP. Starch-based SAP, derived from renewable plant sources, is biodegradable and eco-friendly, making it suitable for sustainable applications. Cellulose-based SAP, sourced from wood fibers, offers good absorption capabilities and biodegradability. Acrylic resin SAP, synthesized from acrylic monomers, possesses superior absorption and retention characteristics, making it ideal for high-performance applications like diapers and medical products. Each type caters to specific market needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
