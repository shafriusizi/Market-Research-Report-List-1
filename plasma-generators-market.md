<p><ul><li>Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD</li><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD</li><li>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD</li><li>Plasma-Enhanced Atomic Layer Deposition (PEALD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plasma generators market consists of several key types used for material deposition and surface modification. Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) involves plasma to enhance chemical reactions for thin film production. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) relies on gaseous reactants to form solid layers. Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) utilizes physical processes like sputtering to deposit materials. Plasma-Enhanced Atomic Layer Deposition (PEALD) combines atomic layer deposition with plasma to achieve precise film thickness and smooth surfaces at low temperatures.</p></p>
