<p><p>Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) encompasses various satellite systems that provide positioning, navigation, and timing services worldwide. The market includes Global Constellation Systems, which like GPS and GLONASS offer global coverage; Regional Constellation Systems, such as EGNOS and WAAS, providing services over specific regions; and Satellite-Based Augmentation Systems (SBAS), enhancing the accuracy and reliability of GNSS signals. Together, these systems facilitate a wide range of applications, from aviation to agriculture, enabling precise localization and navigation across the globe.</p></p>
