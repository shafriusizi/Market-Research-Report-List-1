<p><p>The corrugated paperboard market comprises several types based on structural configurations. Single wall paperboard features one layer of fluted material sandwiched between two liners, making it lightweight and cost-effective for packaging. Double wall paperboard has two layers of fluted material for added strength and durability, suitable for heavier goods. Triple wall paperboard, the strongest variant, consists of three fluted layers, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Other types may include specialized corrugated boards designed for specific industrial or consumer needs.</p></p>
