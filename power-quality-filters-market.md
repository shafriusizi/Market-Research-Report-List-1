<p><ul><li>'Active Filter'</li><li>'Passive Filter'</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power quality filters market is segmented into active filters and passive filters. Active filters utilize power electronics to dynamically adjust and eliminate harmonic disturbances, ensuring minimal disruption to electrical systems. They are adaptive and can respond to varying load conditions. In contrast, passive filters use fixed components like capacitors and inductors to target specific frequency ranges, effectively filtering harmonics but lacking adaptability. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing power quality and improving the efficiency of electrical systems.</p></p>
