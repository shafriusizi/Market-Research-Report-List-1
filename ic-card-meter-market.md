<p><p>The IC Card Meter market is categorized into two main types: Contact Type and Contactless Type. Contact Type meters require physical insertion of the IC card into the meter to facilitate data transfer, ensuring secure interactions and accurate readings. In contrast, Contactless Type meters utilize radio frequency identification (RFID) technology, allowing users to recharge or access data without direct contact. This enhances convenience and efficiency, making Contactless Type increasingly popular in smart metering applications, while both types serve essential roles in utility management.</p></p>
