<p><p>The In-Vitro Cancer Diagnostics market comprises three primary types: instruments, reagents, and kits. Instruments include sophisticated tools used for testing, such as PCR machines and sequencers, which facilitate the analysis of cancer biomarkers. Reagents are chemicals or biological substances that aid in detecting cancer cells or genetic mutations. Kits combine these instruments and reagents into comprehensive packages for specific diagnostic assays, providing healthcare professionals with ready-to-use solutions for accurate cancer detection and monitoring.</p></p>
