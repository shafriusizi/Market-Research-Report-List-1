<p><strong>The Laser-guided Floor Sweepers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"dToF Technology"</li><li>"iToF Technology"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser-guided floor sweepers market is categorized primarily into two types: dToF (direct Time-of-Flight) and iToF (indirect Time-of-Flight) technologies. dToF technology emits laser pulses to measure distance based on direct reflection, providing high accuracy in obstacle detection and mapping. In contrast, iToF technology captures light phase shifts to calculate depth, offering quicker processing suited for dynamic environments. Both technologies enhance operational efficiency and precision in cleaning applications, catering to various industrial and commercial sectors.</p></p>
