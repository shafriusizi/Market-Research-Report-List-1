<p><p>Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) separator batteries are categorized into two primary market types: Deep Cycle and Standby. Deep Cycle AGM batteries are designed for prolonged discharge applications, often used in renewable energy systems and electric vehicles, providing consistent power over extended periods. Standby AGM batteries, on the other hand, are intended for backup power in uninterruptible power supplies (UPS) and telecommunications, delivering reliable, short-term energy supply during outages. Both types capitalize on AGM technology’s low maintenance and spill-resistant features.</p></p>
