<p><strong>The Vane Thermo Anemometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Current Anemometer</li><li>Constant Pressure Anemometer</li><li>Thermostatic Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vane thermo anemometer market is categorized into three main types based on their operational principles. Constant current anemometers maintain a steady current, adjusting the heating power to measure airflow velocity effectively. Constant pressure anemometers operate by regulating the pressure differential across a vane, providing accurate readings of air speeds. Thermostatic anemometers rely on temperature differentials to determine airflow, utilizing the heat loss from a heated element to infer velocity. Each type serves specific applications in various industries, emphasizing precision in airflow measurement.</p></p>
