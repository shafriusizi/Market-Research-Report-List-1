<p><p>The Data Center RFID market can be divided into several key components. Tags are the transponders that store information and can be attached to assets for tracking. Readers are devices that capture data from tags and transmit it to a system for processing. Antennas enhance communication range and efficiency between tags and readers. Other hardware includes supporting equipment like controllers and power supplies. Lastly, the software market encompasses applications for managing and analyzing RFID data to optimize inventory and operational workflows.</p></p>
