<p><p>The Schlieren Imaging System market is categorized primarily into two types: One Mirror and Two Mirror Schlieren Imaging. One Mirror Schlieren systems utilize a single concave mirror to capture and visualize variations in refractive index, making them simpler and more compact. In contrast, Two Mirror Schlieren systems employ two mirrors to enhance image clarity and resolution, allowing for more detailed analysis of fluid flow and thermal effects. Both types serve diverse applications in research, industrial processes, and engineering fields.</p></p>
