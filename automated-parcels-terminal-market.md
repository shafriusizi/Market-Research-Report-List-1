<p><p>Automated Parcel Terminals (APTs) operate in two primary market types: one-sided and double-sided. In a one-sided market, the terminals serve either customers or service providers independently, offering streamlined services to one group without direct interaction with the other. In contrast, double-sided markets facilitate interaction between two distinct user groups, such as senders and receivers, enhancing the overall efficiency of parcel delivery. Both types leverage automation to optimize operations, improve user experience, and increase throughput in logistics.</p></p>
