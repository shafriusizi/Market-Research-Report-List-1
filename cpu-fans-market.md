<p><strong>The CPU Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cooling</li><li>Water Cooling</li><li>Thermoelectric Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPU fan market is categorized into three main types: air cooling, water cooling, and thermoelectric cooling. Air cooling utilizes heat sinks and fans to dissipate heat through airflow, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity. Water cooling employs liquid coolant in a closed loop to efficiently transfer heat away from the CPU, enabling better performance and quieter operation. Thermoelectric cooling uses Peltier devices to create a temperature differential, providing precise cooling, but it is less common due to complexity and cost.</p></p>
