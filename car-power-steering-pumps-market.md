<p><p>The car power steering pump market includes three primary types: blade type, gear type, and plunger type pumps. Blade type steering pumps utilize vanes or blades to create hydraulic pressure, offering smooth and responsive steering. Gear type pumps operate using interlocking gears to generate flow, providing reliability and consistent performance. Plunger type pumps use plungers to create pressure and are known for their compact design and efficiency. Each type serves different vehicle requirements, impacting performance, efficiency, and steering feel.</p></p>
