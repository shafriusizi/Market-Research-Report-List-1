<p><p>The mattress coil market features several types of coil constructions that influence comfort and support. Continuous coils are formed from a single wire, creating a sturdy, uniform support structure. Bonnell coils are hourglass-shaped and widely spaced, offering good durability and airflow. Offset coils have an hourglass shape but feature flat edges for improved support and reduced motion transfer. Marshall coils, or pocket coils, are individually wrapped, allowing for targeted support and minimizing partner disturbance while providing excellent contouring.</p></p>
