<p><ul><li>Single Stage Module</li><li>Multistage Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The miniature thermoelectric generators (TEGs) market is divided into two key types: single-stage modules and multistage modules. Single-stage modules utilize a single thermoelectric element to convert heat directly into electricity, making them simpler and cost-effective for low-temperature applications. In contrast, multistage modules consist of multiple thermoelectric elements stacked together, enhancing efficiency and output by utilizing a wider temperature gradient, suitable for higher energy demands. Each type caters to specific applications, from portable devices to industrial waste heat recovery.</p></p>
