<p><p>The airbag textile market primarily consists of two types: flat airbag fabric and OPW (oven pre-formed woven) airbag fabric. Flat airbag fabric is typically woven into a two-dimensional structure, offering essential protection and deployment characteristics for traditional airbags. In contrast, OPW airbag fabric is engineered using advanced weaving techniques, enabling a more complex, three-dimensional structure that enhances strength and flexibility. Both types are critical for ensuring passenger safety in vehicles, meeting stringent industry standards and performance requirements.</p></p>
