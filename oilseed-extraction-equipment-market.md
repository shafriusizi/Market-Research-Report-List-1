<p><p>The oilseed extraction equipment market comprises various techniques, primarily conventional extraction and ultrasonic extraction. Conventional extraction utilizes mechanical pressing and solvent methods to extract oils from seeds, often focusing on efficiency and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, ultrasonic extraction employs high-frequency sound waves to enhance the oil yield and extraction speed, offering a more environmentally friendly approach. Both methods cater to the growing demand for oilseeds in food, cosmetics, and biofuels, adapting to technological advancements and sustainability trends within the industry.</p></p>
