<p><p>Lithium-ion batteries in the aerospace market are categorized mainly into three types: LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries, Li-NMC (Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt) batteries, and others. LFP batteries are known for their thermal stability and safety, making them suitable for applications requiring reliable operation. Li-NMC batteries offer higher energy density and efficiency, beneficial for weight-sensitive aerospace applications. Other battery types may include variations like lithium polymer and lithium titanate, each serving specific performance needs and optimization for space and weight constraints in aviation.</p></p>
