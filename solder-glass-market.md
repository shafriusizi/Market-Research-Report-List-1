<p><strong>The Solder Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vitreous Solder Glasses</li><li>Devitrifying Solder Glasses</li><li>Composite Solder Glasses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solder glass market comprises three main types: Vitreous solder glasses, which are high-temperature resistant and mainly used for bonding ceramics and glass; Devitrifying solder glasses, which crystallize upon cooling, enhancing mechanical strength but complicating application due to potential changes in properties; and Composite solder glasses, which incorporate various additives to tailor characteristics like thermal expansion and adhesion. Each type serves specific industrial applications in electronics, ceramics, and construction, addressing diverse performance requirements and material compatibility.</p></p>
