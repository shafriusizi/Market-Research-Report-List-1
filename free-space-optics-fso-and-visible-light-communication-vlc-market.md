<p><p>Free Space Optics (FSO) refers to the technology that utilizes light propagating in free space to transmit data wirelessly. It is commonly used for high-speed communications over distances, leveraging laser beams to establish links between buildings or as a backup for fiber optic networks. VLC is particularly valuable in environments where radio frequency communications are limited or undesirable.</p><p>The Free Space Optics (FSO) and Visible Light Communication (VLC) Market is on an upward trajectory, fueled by the increasing demand for high-speed wireless communication and the growth of smart city initiatives.
