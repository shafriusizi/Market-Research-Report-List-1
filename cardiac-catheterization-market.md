<p><strong>The Cardiac Catheterization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angiography Catheters</li><li>Guiding Catheters</li><li>Pulmonary Artery Catheters</li><li>PTCA Balloon Catheters</li><li>Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Catheters</li><li>Electrophysiology Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac catheterization market encompasses various catheter types used in diagnosing and treating heart conditions. Angiography catheters allow visualization of blood vessels, while guiding catheters assist in positioning other devices. Pulmonary artery catheters are employed for monitoring heart pressures. PTCA balloon catheters are crucial for angioplasty procedures, dilating narrowed vessels. Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheters provide detailed imaging of vascular structures, and electrophysiology catheters are used to study and treat electrical activity in the heart, aiding arrhythmia management.</p></p>
