<p><p>Real-Time Location Systems (RTLS) for industrial applications primarily focus on two market segments: systems and tags. Systems encompass the infrastructure and software required to track and manage assets in real-time, often integrating with existing workflows. Tags, on the other hand, are devices attached to assets, enabling their location tracking via various technologies like RFID, GPS, or ultrasound. Together, these components facilitate enhanced inventory management, asset utilization, and operational efficiency in industrial environments.</p></p>
