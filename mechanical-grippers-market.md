<p><p>The mechanical grippers market is primarily divided into electric and pneumatic grippers. Electric grippers utilize electric motors for actuation, offering precise control, ease of integration with automation systems, and reduced maintenance. They are ideal for applications requiring high-speed operation and accuracy. In contrast, pneumatic grippers rely on compressed air to function, providing high gripping force and faster actuation, making them suitable for handling heavier loads. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing productivity and efficiency in automation processes.</p></p>
