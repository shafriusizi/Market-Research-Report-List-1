<p><ul><li>Hot Plate-Type</li><li>Cavity-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blackbody calibrator market includes two primary types: hot plate-type and cavity-type calibrators. Hot plate-type calibrators utilize a heated surface to simulate blackbody radiation, delivering accurate temperature measurements for infrared thermometers. In contrast, cavity-type calibrators achieve higher precision by using a cavity that absorbs and emits thermal energy uniformly. Both types play a crucial role in thermometry and temperature calibration, catering to industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, where precise temperature measurement is critical.</p></p>
