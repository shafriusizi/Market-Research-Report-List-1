<p><p>The bird incubator market is primarily divided into semi-automatic and automatic types. Semi-automatic bird incubators require more manual intervention, such as turning the eggs periodically and monitoring conditions. They are typically more affordable and suitable for small-scale breeders. In contrast, automatic bird incubators offer advanced features like automatic egg turning, temperature, and humidity control, ensuring optimal conditions with minimal user input. This makes them ideal for commercial operations and serious breeders seeking efficiency and consistent hatching results.</p></p>
