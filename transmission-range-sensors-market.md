<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Inductive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transmission Range Sensors market includes Hall Type and Inductive Type sensors. Hall Type sensors use magnetic fields to detect the position and movement of objects, ideal for applications requiring precise positioning. Inductive Type sensors, on the other hand, utilize electromagnetic induction to detect metallic objects without physical contact, making them suitable for harsh environments. Both types are essential for automation, robotics, and automotive industries, enhancing efficiency and safety in various applications by providing reliable distance measurements.</p></p>
