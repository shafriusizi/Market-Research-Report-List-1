<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Probe</li><li>Shear Wave Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical convex ultrasound probe market includes two primary types: longitudinal wave probes and shear wave probes. Longitudinal wave probes utilize sound waves that travel parallel to the direction of propagation, providing effective imaging of soft tissues and organs. In contrast, shear wave probes generate perpendicular waves, which are particularly useful for assessing tissue stiffness and elasticity. Both probe types play crucial roles in diagnostics, enabling healthcare professionals to obtain detailed images for accurate evaluations of various medical conditions.</p></p>
