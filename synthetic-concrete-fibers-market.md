<p><p>The synthetic concrete fibers market is divided into two main types: macro-synthetic fibers and micro-synthetic fibers. Macro-synthetic fibers, typically larger in diameter, enhance the structural integrity of concrete, improving resistance to cracking and enhancing durability. Micro-synthetic fibers, on the other hand, are finer and primarily used to control plastic shrinkage cracking and improve workability during the mixing process. Both types contribute to enhanced performance and longevity of concrete structures, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
