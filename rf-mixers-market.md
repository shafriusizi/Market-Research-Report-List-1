<p><p>RF mixers are essential components in radio frequency applications, designed to convert signals from one frequency to another. The market is primarily divided into active and passive mixers. Active mixers utilize amplifiers to enhance performance and provide better isolation, making them suitable for low signal levels and high-frequency applications. In contrast, passive mixers rely on non-linear components, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but with limitations in gain and isolation. Both types cater to diverse needs in communication, broadcasting, and radar systems.</p></p>
