<p><p>The respiratory heater market comprises two main types: benchtop and portable respiratory heaters. Benchtop respiratory heaters are designed for stationary use in clinical settings, providing reliable warming of gases for patient comfort and compliance during ventilation procedures. In contrast, portable respiratory heaters are lightweight and designed for mobility, making them suitable for emergency situations or home care settings. Both types enhance respiratory therapy by ensuring that inspired air is warmed and humidified, improving patient outcomes and overall comfort.</p></p>
