<p><strong>The DC Brushless Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface type Magnetic Pole</li><li>Embedded type Magnetic Pole</li><li>Circular Magnetic Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC brushless motors market is categorized into three main types based on magnetic pole configuration. Surface type magnetic pole motors feature windings on the stator and magnets affixed to the rotor's surface, providing high torque and efficiency. Embedded type magnetic pole motors have magnets embedded in the rotor, enhancing compactness and reducing cogging torque. Circular magnetic pole motors utilize a circular arrangement of magnetic poles, optimizing performance for specific applications, ensuring smooth operation and improved thermal characteristics. Each type caters to distinct consumer needs.</p></p>
