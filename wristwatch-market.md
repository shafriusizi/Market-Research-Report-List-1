<p><p>The wristwatch market is primarily categorized into mechanical and electronic watches. Mechanical watches rely on intricate gear systems powered by springs, appealing to enthusiasts who appreciate craftsmanship and traditional techniques. They often feature automatic or manual winding mechanisms. Electronic watches, on the other hand, utilize quartz movements powered by batteries, offering high accuracy and low maintenance. These timepieces are popular for their affordability and technological features, such as digital displays and smart functionalities, catering to a broader audience.</p></p>
