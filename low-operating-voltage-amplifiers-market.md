<p><p>Low Operating Voltage Amplifiers are categorized into three main types: Single Operator, Dual Operator, and Quad Operator. Single Operator amplifiers are designed for applications requiring one amplification channel, ideal for compact spaces. Dual Operator amplifiers offer two amplification channels, providing versatility and efficiency for more complex circuits. Quad Operator amplifiers feature four channels, enabling even higher integration and reduced board space, suitable for multi-channel applications. Each type caters to specific design needs and constraints, enhancing performance while minimizing power consumption.</p></p>
