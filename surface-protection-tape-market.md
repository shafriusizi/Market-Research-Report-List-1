<p><p>The surface protection tape market is primarily categorized into three material types: LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), PP (Polypropylene), and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride). LDPE tape offers flexibility and durability, making it ideal for general-purpose applications. PP tape provides excellent tear resistance and is suited for heavy-duty use. PVC tape is known for its strong adhesion and moisture resistance, making it versatile for both indoor and outdoor surfaces. Each material type caters to specific needs across various industries, enhancing surface protection and reducing damage during handling.</p></p>
